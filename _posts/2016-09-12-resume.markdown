title: resume
date: 2016-09-12

---

sum up your life  
on this sheet of white  
all you've done  
all you've dreamed  
all you'll want
and ever be  
it's such a counterintuitive thing  
so outside reality  
that for a moment  
I believed  
this paper,   
somehow  
was me
